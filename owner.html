About the Owner

Harshdeep Singh

Birthplace: Lucknow, India Date of Birth: May 12, 1993

Harshdeep Singh, a native of Lucknow, embodies the spirit of innovation and dedication in the realm of manufacturing. Born and raised amidst the vibrant culture of Lucknow, he cultivated a passion for technology and engineering from an early age. His journey into the world of entrepreneurship began with a solid foundation in education. Harshdeep pursued his Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Amity University, Lucknow. Throughout his academic tenure, he exhibited a remarkable aptitude for problem-solving and a keen interest in cutting-edge technologies. After completing his education, Harshdeep Singh ventured into the dynamic landscape of manufacturing. With a vision to revolutionize the industry, he founded the pet preform making factory situated in the heart of Lucknow. His entrepreneurial spirit, coupled with his expertise in technology, became the driving force behind the factory's success.

Factory Management:


Harshdeep Singh's leadership style is characterized by a meticulous attention to detail and a relentless pursuit of excellence. As the owner of the pet preform making factory, he plays a pivotal role in overseeing every aspect of operations. From optimizing production processes to ensuring stringent quality control measures, Harshdeep's hands-on approach ensures the factory operates at peak efficiency. Moreover, his ability to inspire and empower his team fosters a culture of innovation and collaboration within the factory premises. Under his guidance, the factory has not only achieved operational excellence but has also emerged as a beacon of reliability and customer satisfaction in the industry. Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, Harshdeep Singh continues to lead the factory towards new heights of success, cementing its position as a leader in the field of pet preform manufacturing.
